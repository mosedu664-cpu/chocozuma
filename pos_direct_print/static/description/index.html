Get a Direct Print Solution

Odoo POS Direct Print Module simplifies the process of printing receipts, making it straightforward and hassle-free. You can easily print and re-print order receipts without needing subscription services. Perfect for businesses looking for a dependable, non-network-based solution, this module keeps your printing running smoothly, even if the printer goes offline.

Simplify Your Printing Process

With the Odoo POS Direct Print Module, you can avoid the complicated setups and network hassles. Print directly from your POS system without needing network or kitchen printers. If your printer goes offline, the module keeps track of print commands and processes them as soon as the printer is back online, so your operations keep running smoothly, even in challenging situations.

Print Without Limits

Escape the limitations of old-school POS printing with the Odoo POS Direct Print Module. Eliminate the hassle of network dependencies and subscription fees, giving your business the flexibility to print receipts easily. Whether your printer is online or offline, you’ll keep things running smoothly and keep your customers happy.
